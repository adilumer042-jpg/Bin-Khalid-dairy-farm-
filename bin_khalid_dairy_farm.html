✦ Our Story

A Legacy of Pure,
Natural Dairy

Bin Khalid Dairy Farm has been serving the people of Lahore with the finest quality milk and fresh meat for over 15 years. Located on the outskirts of Lahore, our farm spans acres of lush green land where our cattle graze freely in natural conditions.

We believe in traditional farming values combined with modern hygiene standards. Every product that leaves our farm is fresh, pure, and full of nutrition — straight from our farm to your table.
